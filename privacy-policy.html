This Privacy Policy describes the policies of the "Marcador bosnia" Chrome extension regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information. The extension is developed by erick789paul and is designed to automatically create bookmarks for PayPal pages in your browser's bookmark bar.

Information Collection and Use

The "Marcador bosnia" extension does not collect, store, or process any personal data or usage information. It solely creates bookmarks in your browser and does not access your browsing history, IP address, device information, or any other personal identifiable information.

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions to function:

  • bookmarks: To create and manage bookmarks automatically.
  • windows: To interact with browser windows for bookmark management.
These permissions are used exclusively for the purpose of creating bookmarks and do not involve data collection.

Data Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties, service providers, or affiliates, as no data is collected by the extension.
